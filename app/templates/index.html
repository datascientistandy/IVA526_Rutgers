Welcome to your weather companion

Weathering Change


This app combines historical weather data, predictive modeling,
and community-driven insights to help you better understand and
prepare for today’s weather and tomorrow’s climate.
Whether you're looking to explore the human cost of weather disasters,
understand how climate has shifted in your region, or contribute
your own observations, this platform brings data to life—and puts
climate awareness in your hands.
